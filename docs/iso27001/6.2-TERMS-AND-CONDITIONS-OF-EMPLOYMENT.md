# Terms and Conditions of Employment
**ISO/IEC 27001:2022 — Control 6.2**
**MayWin Nurse Scheduling Platform**
Last reviewed: 2026-03-30

---

## Purpose
Ensure all personnel understand their information security responsibilities as part of their engagement with the MayWin platform.

## Security Obligations for All Users

All users of the MayWin platform agree to the following upon account activation:

1. **Acceptable Use**: Use the system solely for authorized scheduling purposes as defined in `src/ACCEPTABLE_USE_POLICY.md`.
2. **Confidentiality**: Not disclose nurse personal data, schedules, or system credentials to unauthorized parties.
3. **Credential Security**: Not share login credentials, JWT tokens, or OTP codes with others.
4. **Incident Reporting**: Report suspected security incidents immediately to the Technical Lead or `ORG_ADMIN`.
5. **Data Handling**: Not export, copy, or retain system data beyond what is required for job duties.
6. **Compliance**: Comply with applicable data protection law (Thailand PDPA and any applicable equivalent).

## Staff-Specific Obligations (ORG_ADMIN / UNIT_MANAGER)
- Ensure nurses under their management are aware of their data rights and system use expectations.
- Promptly deactivate accounts for staff who leave (`DELETE /api/v1/core/staff/:id`).
- Not create user accounts for individuals who have not been screened per Control 6.1.

## Acknowledgement
Users acknowledge these terms when their account is created. The welcome email includes a reference to the Acceptable Use Policy.

## Consequences of Breach
Violations are recorded in the audit log. Serious violations may result in account deactivation and escalation to hospital administration.
