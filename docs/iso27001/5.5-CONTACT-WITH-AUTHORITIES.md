# Contact with Authorities
**ISO/IEC 27001:2022 — Control 5.5**
**MayWin Nurse Scheduling Platform**
Last reviewed: 2026-03-30

---

## Purpose
Maintain appropriate contacts with relevant authorities to ensure timely communication during security incidents, legal obligations, or regulatory enquiries.

## Applicable Authorities

| Authority | Contact Trigger | Action |
|---|---|---|
| National cybersecurity authority (e.g. NCSA Thailand / ETDA) | Data breach affecting personal data | Notify within 72 hours per PDPA requirements |
| Law enforcement | Evidence of criminal activity (e.g. unauthorized access) | Preserve logs, contact police cybercrime unit |
| Cloud provider (AWS) | Suspected account compromise | Contact AWS Support via Trusted Advisor |
| Data Protection Officer (DPO) | Any PII breach | Internal escalation within 24 hours |
| LINE Corporation | Compromise of LINE Bot credentials | Revoke and regenerate via LINE Developers Console |

## Personal Data (PDPA / GDPR Alignment)
The MayWin platform processes nurse personal data (name, email, LINE ID, work schedules). Any breach of this data must be reported to the relevant data protection authority within the timeframe required by applicable law.

## Process
1. Incident identified → log in `INCIDENT_RESPONSE_PLAN.md` process.
2. Technical Lead assesses whether regulatory notification is required.
3. If required, notify the relevant authority using contact details maintained by the project owner.
4. Document all communications in the incident record.
